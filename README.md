# Blinkit-Data-Analysis-Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end data analysis of Blinkit’s grocery operations using Power BI. The objective is to evaluate product performance, outlet efficiency, sales trends, and customer satisfaction, and translate data into actionable business insights.

## Business Problem
Blinkit faces multiple operational challenges:
- Underperforming product categories despite demand
- Variability in outlet performance across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities
- Impact of item visibility and fat content on sales
- Need to monitor customer satisfaction and improve retention

## Dataset Description
The dataset consists of three main components:
- **Item Details:** Item Type, Fat Content, Visibility, Weight
- **Sales Data:** Sales, Rating
- **Outlet Details:** Outlet Type, Size, Tier, Establishment Year

## Data Preparation
- Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
- Handled missing values and ensured data consistency
- Removed duplicates and standardized formats

## DAX Measures
Key metrics created using DAX:
- Total Sales
- Average Sales per Outlet
- Average Customer Rating (CSAT)
- No. of Items
  
## Dashboard Features
### KPI Cards:
- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Customer Rating
- No. of Items 
### Visualizations:
- Bar Charts → Sales by Category & Outlet Type
- Line Chart → Sales Trend over Years
- Donut Charts → Fat Content & Outlet Size Distribution
- Matrix/Table → Top 10 Items
### Interactivity:
- Slicers (Tier, Outlet Type, Item Type, Fat Content)
- Drill-through navigation
- Conditional formatting for performance insights

## Key Insights
- Fruits, Snack Foods, and Household categories drive the highest sales.
- Low-fat products outperform regular items, indicating health-conscious demand.
- Tier 2 outlets and Supermarket Type1 generate the highest revenue.
- Sales show initial growth followed by stagnation, indicating market saturation.
- High visibility but low sales in grocery stores highlight conversion inefficiencies.
- 
## Business Recommendations
- Expand high-performing categories to maximize revenue
- Focus on Tier 2 market expansion strategies
- Improve product placement and pricing in grocery stores
- Introduce targeted promotions for underperforming products
